WHY COMPUTER SCIENCE

Every time I open Google Maps for navigation, I'm reminded of the days when I used to lose track of my journey and had to stop frequently to pester the locals for directions. Similarly, there are booming solutions for large holes in everyday requirements. However, besides being large, the requirements are also ever-expanding. It is in this context computer science has become the channel for holistic solutions. I’ve always been enthralled that this field substitutes for years of manpower and cerebration with algorithms and machines. While working on a problem statement that elicited an amalgamation of two disparate domains - The Internet of Things and Machine Learning - I understood how germane and optimized solutions can stem from the combination of various facets in this field. This fascination lays a solid foundation for my desire to pursue a master’s degree to deepen my knowledge and practical expertise in computer science.
